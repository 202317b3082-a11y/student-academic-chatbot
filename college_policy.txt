Dear student, Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA): The CGPA (calculated on a 10 point scale) will be used to describe the overall performance of a student (from the semester of admission till the point of reckoning) in all courses for which LETTER GRADES are awarded. U1*G1 + U2*G2 + U3*G3 …….. CGPA = ___________________________ U1 + U2 + U3 ..... Where U1, U2, U3 …… denote UNITS of the course, and G1, G2, G3 denote grade points of the LETTER GRADES awarded in that course. Non-Letter grades do not go into the computation of CGPA.

Dear Alumnus, The normal procedure for authentication of degree certificates by the Department of Higher Education (DHE), MHRD, Government of Rajasthan (for universities whose headquarters is located in Rajasthan) is as follows: 1. The student concerned downloads the application form from the DHE's website, and submits the duly completed form along with a copy of his or her degree certificate. https://hte.rajasthan.gov.in/ 2. The DHE sends a scanned copy of the degree certificate by email to the university concerned for verification. 3. The university concerned (BITS Pilani, in this case) should duly complete a prescribed form available in DHE website to certify that the student concerned was indeed a bonafide student of the university who has successfully completed the programme and has graduated with the specified degree, and also verify the copy of the degree certificate, and send the documents by email back to DHE. 4. Based on the information and verification received from the university concerned, DHE may decide to authenticate the degree certificate, and then call the student or his representative in person to the DHE office located in the State Secretariat, Government of Rajasthan, Jaipur with the original degree certificate for attestation. 5. Once the degree certificate is authenticated by DHE, the student may submit it to the embassy of the nation concerned for attestation, which would attest the degree certificate and enable the student to pursue professional or educational opportunities in the country concerned . BITS Pilani would follow the due procedure for verification of degree certificates as specified by the Government of Rajasthan. Regards
